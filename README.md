Botswana Economic Growth Intelligence Dashboard
Project Overview

The Botswana Economic Growth Intelligence Dashboard is a Business Intelligence and Economic Intelligence platform developed to transform economic data into actionable insights for policymakers, investors, researchers, and decision-makers.

Using data sourced from Statistics Botswana, the platform integrates economic performance analysis, shock intelligence, diversification monitoring, opportunity assessment, resilience measurement, and forecasting capabilities into a unified decision-support system.

The project moves beyond traditional economic reporting by applying Business Intelligence methodologies to convert raw economic data into strategic intelligence capable of supporting evidence-based decision-making.
